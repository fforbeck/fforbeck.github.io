\centered **AMENDMENT NO. 3 TO THE NACCO MATERIALS HANDLING GROUP, INC. LONG-TERM INCENTIVE COMPENSATION PLAN FOR THE PERIOD FROM [[Date 2: Date]] THROUGH [[Date 1: Date]]**  **(As Amended and Restated as of [[Date 1]])** 

NACCO Materials Handling Group, Inc. hereby adopts this Amendment No. 3 to the NACCO Materials Handling Group, Inc. Long-Term Incentive Compensation Plan For the Period from January 1, 2000 through [[Date 1]] (As Amended and Restated as of December 31, 2007) (the “Plan”), to be effective as of [[Date 3: Date]]. Words used herein with initial capital letters which are defined in the Plan are used herein as so defined. 

\centered Section 1 

Section 13 of the Plan is hereby amended by adding the following new Subsection (d) to the end thereof, to read as follows: 

“(d) Notwithstanding the foregoing, or any other provision of the Plan to the contrary, NACCO Industries, Inc. shall be responsible for the payment of the amounts credited to the 2007 Sub-Accounts of all Participants other than Non-U.S. Participants (including interest) that are due to be paid during the period from [[Date 4: Date]] and [[Date 5: Date]]. In furtherance thereof, but without limiting the foregoing, NACCO Industries, Inc. shall not be responsible for the payment of any other amount under the Plan.”
